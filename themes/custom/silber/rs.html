Welcome to Research Support

Our new one-stop resource designed to simplify and support research activity at BU

Search

Helping you navigate the entire project lifecycle

The business of doing research is not always linear. We get that. Consider this the one-stop support network for the entire BU research community, from undergrads to postdocs to faculty and staff. Whether you’re applying for, managing, or closing out an award, or looking to commercialize your invention, we’ve got you covered with the most up-to-date and accurate information. We also have plenty of human resources on hand if you have questions or need guidance. Safety concerns? Export Control? Special circumstances? We’re ready to handle it all, right here. Please dive in!
